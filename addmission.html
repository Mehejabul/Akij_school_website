A Brief Addmission Procedure

Once the admission opening date has been decided, Parents can call, email for the admission information or may come to the School Campus & complete the prescribed query form regarding admission.

Step 1Issuance of Prospectus

Step 2Parent to meet admission office to get details of admission procedure, fee schedule etc. After approval of MD & CEO/Principal/Vice Principal, parents can obtain the prescribed admission form from the Principal’s Office at a particular cost.

Step 3 Oral Interaction (with parents and student): Pre-Primary

Step 4 Written test for KG-I to Class VIII.

Step 5 Admission orders – An SMS of admission given to the parents

Step 6 Fees to be deposited in the Accounts Department or in mentioned Bank

Step 7 Parent has to fill up the required details to complete the admission form and submit the form. Recent passport size photograph of student & parent, a copy of child’s birth certificate, recent report cards photocopy must be submitted along with admission form to the Admission Office.

Step 8 Admission Office will accept admission applications within a deadline (or on a seat-available basis)

Step 9 Uniform, books, stationery etc. to be arranged, by the parents according to the given instructions.

Step 10 Join AIS orientation & be a proud member of AIS Family.

Admission into Play Group

A child who will be of 3 years’ age in the month of July/August of the current year will be eligible.

Admission into Nursery

A child who will be of 4 years’ age in the month of July/August ofthe current year and who has completed one year of Play Group inan institution equivalent to AIS will be eligible.

Admission into KG-I to class-VIII

Admission test is necessary for every one, even if copies of appropriate report cards, showing satisfactory achievement, are submitted.

Admission into A’Level

For entry into class XI a copy of the results in the IGCSE O’Level examination or a copy of the admit card for appearing at IGCSE O’ Level examination (whichever is appropriate) in at least six subjects including English Language, Bangla Language and Mathematics – B will be required.
