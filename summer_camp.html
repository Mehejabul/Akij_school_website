1. Pottery by Clay Station

Ceramics has unlimited life and the option to make beautiful pieces of art that are sustainable, fun and creative. The course is designed and conducted by Clay Station, which is an open studio for rejuvenating one’s soul. This is an opportunity for social interaction, creative exploration and simple hands-on play with clay. Sign up for Pottery. It is being offered for the first time at this year’s Glenrich Senior School Summer Camp.

2. Archery

Archery builds focus and concentration while being a very fun activity. It is a great way to socialize and strengthen self-confidence to create a goal-oriented mindset among the archers. For the first time, Glenrich School Dhaka is bringing you the activity of archery, and it will be conducted by a national-level archery player from the Bangladesh Archery Foundation. Only this activity of Summer Camp 2024 will cost BDT6,000/-, as the cost of arrows is included. Sign up for this unique activity, which is being offered for the first time at this year’s Summer Camp.

3. Swimming

The water is your friend. One does not have to fight with water but just share the same spirit as the creatures of the water, and it will help them become at one with the water. Glenrich School Dhaka’s Olympic-standard swimming pool at the Senior School campus is ready for you, so you can swim countless laps on it and become the athlete that you were meant to be. Our professional swimming coach is ready to make you a master of swimming. Don’t miss the opportunity to cool off in our pool during this sweltering summer.

4. Football by The Growing Up Club

Football Activity this year will be run by The Growing Up Club which is a community for children in Bangladesh to explore different activities that promote a healthy lifestyle, creativity and leadership. The aim is to provide a holistic set of programs that will help children live happy, healthy and confident lives, and equip them with the skills needed to survive in this ever changing world. Don’t miss out on playing Football in this year’s Summer Camp, if you want to be the next international football star.

5. Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a martial art that originated in Korea and has since become one of the most popular forms of martial arts in the world. It is a full-body workout that combines cardio, strength training, and flexibility. Taekwondo is also a great way to improve self-defense skills. The techniques taught in Taekwondo classes are designed to protect oneself from potential attackers. Sign up for Taekwondo activity, which is back at Summer Camp after a hiatus. Our highly qualified Taekwondo coach will teach you how to be a master. So sign up to be a master.

7. Basketball

In 2024, Glenrich School Dhaka’s Basketball Team “The Mustangs” became the Champions in both boys’ and girls’ categories in the Glenrich Inter-school Basketball Tournament, while beating institutions such as Scholastica, Aga Khan Academy, International Hope School Bangladesh, Rajuk College, and many others. This means Glenrich Mustangs is one of the top school basketball teams in the country currently. If you want to be the next Mustang, then you must sign up for Basketball in the Senior School Summer Camp.

8. Cricket - Coach from CricKingdom Academy by Rohit Sharma

This time cricket activity of Glenrich Senior School Summer Camp will be nothing you have seen before. We are bringing a cricket coach from the CricKingdom Academy by Rohit Sharma to coach you in such a way that basic cricketing skills get instilled within you firmly. All you need to do after that is regularly practice the basics, and slowly your cricket career will take off. Don’t miss out on this opportunity to get trained by a professional coach from CricKingdom Academy by Rohit Sharma.

9. Life Skills by Don Sumdany

A very new Summer Activity is being introduced this year called Life Skills, and it will be taken by celebrity trainer Don Sumdany. The participants of the activity will be taught essential life skills such as public speaking, social skills, social media awareness, managing emotions, money management, time management, being self-starters and self-motivated, how to navigate relationships with family and friends, and many other amazing life skills. Don’t miss this valuable opportunity.

Who can express life better than an artist and a craftsman? Let your imagination go wild, and express your thoughts through your drawings, colourings, paintings, sketching, crafting and all associated fun activities. The tools and tricks imparted by our Art and Craft teachers can improve your skills as an artist who draws, sketches and paints on canvas, and can make fun and aesthetic things with their hands. Join our Summer Camp and express your creativity.
